Estimate output, fuel cost, stacks per smoker, total stacks, gross revenue, net profit, time to 1B, and the setup needed to hit a target money goal.

Inputs

Why these inputs matter

  • Sell price: server economy can change.
  • Blaze rod cost: directly changes net profit.
  • Growth tick time: real farm-side output depends on server behavior and lag.
  • Smoker timing: determines your hard throughput ceiling.
  • Target money + target days: calculates the balanced setup needed to hit your goal.
